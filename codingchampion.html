Description

'Coding Junior' is an exciting course designed to introduce kids to the world of coding. This course is perfect for children who are interested in learning how to create animations, build apps and games, and explore the possibilities of technology. Throughout the course, children will learn the basics of coding using visual programming languages such as Scratch and Blockly, which are easy to use and understand. They will learn how to create animations, build interactive games, and develop their own apps. The course is designed to be fun and engaging, with a mix of interactive activities, hands-on projects, and group discussions. Children will be encouraged to think creatively and experiment with different coding concepts, while also learning important problem-solving skills. By the end of the course, children will have a solid understanding of coding and will be able to create their own animations, apps, and games. They will also have the confidence to continue learning and exploring the world of coding on their own. Coding Junior is a great way to introduce children to the exciting world of technology and to foster a love of learning that will serve them well throughout their lives.
